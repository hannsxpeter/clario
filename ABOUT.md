# About Clario

## The one idea

AI made marketing execution free. Anyone can generate a thousand ads, emails, and landing pages before lunch. That is exactly why execution stopped being an advantage, and why the market started punishing it. Consumers can smell generic AI copy, and they trust it less. Verified human trust became the scarce thing.

Clario is built on a single move: **generate at AI speed without sounding like AI.** It learns a brand's real voice, then refuses to ship anything that reads like a machine wrote it.

## Humanize marketing

The whole product leans toward storytelling over feature-listing, and toward a human voice over polished filler. That is not a slogan bolted on at the end. It is enforced in three concrete places:

1. **Company DNA** captures the brand's actual character (its story: the origin, the villain it fights, the transformation it promises), not a generic persona template. Story is what makes copy feel human.
2. **The humanize-and-score loop** runs every customer-facing asset through an authenticity check and a voice-matching rewrite. The score is visible. If it does not read human, you see it, and Clario fixes it.
3. **Honest numbers.** The plan never invents precision. ROI and success come as ranges with assumptions, forecasts are labeled as forecasts, and selection effects are flagged. A tool that says "estimated 3 to 5x ROAS, assuming X" reads as credible. One that says "412% ROI" reads as the slop we are trying to avoid.

## Standing on my own work

Clario is not a from-scratch guess at what "brand voice" means. It composes four skills I have already published and battle-tested, ported here into server-side prompts:

- **scriveno** gives the voice model. Its Voice DNA profiles a writer across a 9-part, 15+ dimension style guide. Clario applies that same rigor to a company.
- **authenticity-check** gives the score. A 32-pattern catalog of AI tells across six families, a four-pass method, and a banded 0 to 100 result.
- **humanizer** gives the rewrite. The same catalog, run in reverse, to make text sound like a specific voice again.
- **voiceprint** gives the loop. Diagnose once, rewrite once, report. No detector-gaming, no infinite polishing.

The recommendation engine is grounded in a verified "Future of Marketing in the AI Era" research base I fact-checked claim by claim. Its honesty ethos is baked into Clario's prompts.

## Who it is for

It's Today Media buys media at scale to build email and SMS lists, on Google, Meta, Taboola, and TikTok. A performance shop lives on creative volume and on creative that converts. Clario gives both: the speed of AI generation, with a human voice that a fatigued, AI-wary audience will actually trust. That is the edge, and it is getting rarer by the month.
